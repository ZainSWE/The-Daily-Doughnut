3. Doughnut Party

Edmonton, Alberta

Doughnut Party is one of the most exciting doughnut shops in Canada, known for its unique flavors and artistic presentation. It was established back in 2017 and has quickly grown into a must-visit spot for doughnut enthusiasts. What started as a single shop in Edmonton has expanded to four locations-three in Edmonton and one in Calgary. They've built a loyal following by constantly keeping things fresh with a rotating weekly menu, meaning every time you visit, there's something new to try. The downside? If you fall in love with a flavor, you might have to wait for it to come back. One thing that makes Doughnut Party different is how they approach their flavors. They don't just stick to the usual chocolate or vanilla; they go all out with bold colors, crazy toppings, and combinations that somehow just work. Every doughnut is made fresh daily, and they always have a fun, modern aesthetic that makes them just as nice to look at as they are to eat. Some of my top picks from Doughnut Party's lineup:

  • Vanilla Dipped Lucky Charms:

    A throwback to childhood, featuring a smooth vanilla glaze topped with crunchy, colorful lucky charms cereal.

  • Mothership:

    A rich, chocolatey masterpiece that feels like a dessert out of this world (quite literally).

  • Candy Conucopia:

    A playful, seasonal creation perfect for those who enjoy a mix of flavors.

If you're ever in Edmonton or Calgary and want a doughnut that's anything but boring, Doughnut Party is definitely worth a stop. Whether you're into the classics or want something completely new, they've got something that will surprise you.

2. Cartems Donuts

Vancouver, British Columbia

Cartems Donuts is hands down one of the best places in Vancouver to grab a fresh, high-quality doughnut. They opened in 2012 with a focus on using organic, locally sourced ingredients, and that commitment to quality is a big part of why they've been so successful. Unlike a lot of doughnut shops, Cartems makes everything from scratch, ensuring every bite is soft, flavorful, and totally worth it. They also cater to different dietary needs, offering vegan and gluten-free options that are just as good as the regular ones. The name "Cartems" doesn't really mean anything specific, but the shop has definitely built a meaning around it-quality, creativity, and a welcoming vibe. Beyond the doughnuts, the shop itself has a super laid-back, friendly atmosphere, and you can tell they care about the community. They frequently collaborate with local artists and businesses, making it more than just a place to grab a snack. Some of their standout flavors include:

  • Blueberry Cornbread:

    A perfect mix of sweet and savory, with a soft cornbread base and juicy blueberries baked in.

  • White Chocolate Raspberry:

    A decadent doughnut topped with creamy white chocolate and a tangy raspberry drizzle.

  • Vanilla Bean Glaze with Oreo Sprinkles

    A wild combo of toasted marshmallow, chocolate, and a citrusy creamsicle twist.

Cartems isn't just about making doughnuts; it's about creating an experience. If you're in Vancouver and want something fresh, unique, and made with care, this is the place to go.

1. SuzyQ Doughnuts

Ottawa, Ontario

SuzyQ Doughnuts is one of Ottawa's most well-known and beloved doughnut shops. They started small, running out of a converted shipping container in 2012, but word spread quickly, and they soon moved into a larger space. What sets SuzyQ apart is their signature doughnut base, inspired by a Finnish recipe that makes them incredibly light and fluffy. This unique texture, combined with their bold flavors, is what keeps people coming back. The shop focuses on quality ingredients and keeps a balance between classic flavors and creative new ones. They have staple flavors that are always available, but they also introduce seasonal and limited-time options to keep things interesting. Whether you like simple glazed doughnuts or something totally out there, SuzyQ has something for everyone. Some of their must-try flavors:

  • Black Forest:

    A chocolate lover's dream, loaded with rich layers of cherry and cream.

  • Carrot Cake:

    A spiced doughnut topped with a luscious cream cheese glaze that actually tastes like homemade carrot cake.

  • Vanilla Bean Glaze with Oreo Sprinkles:

    A simple, yet perfect combo of smooth vanilla glaze and crunchy Oreo pieces.

SuzyQ has grown from a small operation to a key part of Ottawa's food scene, and it's easy to see why. The doughnuts are fresh, made with care, and absolutely delicious. If you're ever in Ottawa and want to treat yourself to a top-tier doughnut, this is the place to go.
